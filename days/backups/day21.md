<h3 id="atami-tokyo-transition">From Coastal Resort to Urban Innovation</h3>

     <h4>About Ramen Jiro</h4>
     <p>Ramen Jiro is a cult phenomenon in Japan, known for its distinctive style that's quite different from typical ramen. The Mita location is the original shop that started the chain. What makes it unique? Enormous portions featuring thick, chewy noodles, heavy on garlic and fat, topped with mountains of bean sprouts and slices of fatty pork. It's an experience as much as a meal, and finishing a bowl is considered an achievement!</p>
