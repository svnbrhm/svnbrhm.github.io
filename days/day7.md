<details class="section-details">
  <summary class="section-summary">Main Activity: Scuba Diving (Full Morning / Early Afternoon)</summary>
  <div class="section-content-wrapper">

<details>
  <summary><strong>Kerama Islands Diving Experience</strong></summary>
  <div class="details-content-wrapper">
  <h4>Activity Info</h4>
   <ul>
    <li><i class="fas fa-info-circle fa-fw"></i> <span class="detail-label">About:</span> <span class="detail-value">Explore the famously clear "Kerama Blue" waters. Typical boat trips include 2 or 3 dives at different sites around the islands, known for vibrant coral reefs, diverse fish life, and high chances of encountering sea turtles.</span></li>
    <li><i class="far fa-clock fa-fw"></i> <span class="detail-label">Duration:</span> <span class="detail-value">Likely a half-day trip, returning around lunchtime or early afternoon (Confirm exact schedule with dive operator).</span></li>
    <li><i class="fas fa-yen-sign fa-fw"></i> <span class="detail-label">Price Estimate:</span> <span class="detail-value">Approx. ¥15,000 - ¥20,000 / €88.76 - €118.34 for a 2/3 dive trip including rental gear. **Confirm final price and inclusions with operator.**</span></li>
    <li><i class="fas fa-question-circle fa-fw"></i> <span class="detail-label">Confirmations:</span> <span class="detail-value">Ensure you have confirmed meeting time, location, gear rental needs, included lunch (see below), and payment method with your chosen dive operator.</span></li>
  </ul>
  <h4>Kerama Marine Life</h4>
  <p>The Kerama Islands are known for exceptional biodiversity. You might encounter:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><i class="fas fa-fish fa-fw"></i> <span class="detail-label">Coral:</span> <span class="detail-value">Over 200 species of coral in vibrant colors</span></li>
    <li><i class="fas fa-fish fa-fw"></i> <span class="detail-label">Fish:</span> <span class="detail-value">Tropical reef fish, schools of fusiliers, clownfish in anemones</span></li>
    <li><i class="fas fa-fish fa-fw"></i> <span class="detail-label">Sea Turtles:</span> <span class="detail-value">Green sea turtles are commonly spotted</span></li>
    <li><i class="fas fa-fish fa-fw"></i> <span class="detail-label">Seasonal:</span> <span class="detail-value">Manta rays (winter), nudibranchs, occasional reef sharks</span></li>
  </ul>
  <h4>Diving Safety</h4>
  <ul>
    <li><i class="fas fa-heartbeat fa-fw"></i> <span class="detail-label">Hydration:</span> <span class="detail-value">Drink plenty of water before and between dives</span></li>
    <li><i class="fas fa-plane fa-fw"></i> <span class="detail-label">Flying:</span> <span class="detail-value">Remember the 24-hour no-fly rule after diving</span></li>
    <li><i class="fas fa-sun fa-fw"></i> <span class="detail-label">Sun Protection:</span> <span class="detail-value">Apply reef-safe sunscreen between dives</span></li>
  </ul>
