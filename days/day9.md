  <summary class="section-summary">Main Activity: Scuba Diving (Full Morning / Early Afternoon)</summary>
  <div class="section-content-wrapper">

<details>
  <summary><strong>Tokashiki Diving Experience</strong></summary>
  <div class="details-content-wrapper">
  <h4>Activity Info</h4>
   <ul>
    <li><i class="fas fa-info-circle fa-fw"></i> <span class="detail-label">About:</span> <span class="detail-value">Explore dive sites around Tokashiki Island, known for clear Kerama Blue waters, healthy coral reefs, diverse marine life, and potential sea turtle sightings. Pension Sea Friend runs boat trips directly from the Aharen area.</span></li>
    <li><i class="far fa-clock fa-fw"></i> <span class="detail-label">Duration:</span> <span class="detail-value">Typically 2 or 3 dives, returning around lunchtime or early afternoon (Confirm schedule with Sea Friend).</span></li>
    <li><i class="fas fa-yen-sign fa-fw"></i> <span class="detail-label">Price Estimate:</span> <span class="detail-value">Approx. ¥15,000 - ¥20,000 / €88.76 - €118.34 for a 2/3 dive trip including rental gear. **Confirm final price and inclusions directly with Sea Friend.**</span></li>
  </ul>
  <h4>Tokashiki Dive Sites</h4>
  <p>Tokashiki Island features numerous excellent dive sites, including:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><i class="fas fa-water fa-fw"></i> <span class="detail-label">Aharen Drop-off:</span> <span class="detail-value">Wall dive with coral gardens and diverse marine life</span></li>
    <li><i class="fas fa-water fa-fw"></i> <span class="detail-label">Turtle Point:</span> <span class="detail-value">Known for frequent sea turtle sightings</span></li>
    <li><i class="fas fa-water fa-fw"></i> <span class="detail-label">Chibishi Islands:</span> <span class="detail-value">Series of uninhabited islets with pristine reefs</span></li>
    <li><i class="fas fa-water fa-fw"></i> <span class="detail-label">Ama Garden:</span> <span class="detail-value">Shallow site with abundant soft corals</span></li>
  </ul>
  <h4>Marine Life</h4>
  <p>You might encounter:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Green sea turtles and hawksbill turtles</li>
    <li>Colorful reef fish including clownfish, butterflyfish, and parrotfish</li>
    <li>Nudibranchs and other macro life</li>
    <li>Occasional reef sharks, rays, and schools of jacks</li>
    <li>Diverse hard and soft corals</li>
  </ul>
